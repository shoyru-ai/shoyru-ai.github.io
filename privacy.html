Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Shoyru's Addon Suite (the "Software") and the website at shoyru.app (the "Site") collect, how we use it, and your choices. We designed the Software to collect as little as possible: it has no ads, no third-party advertising trackers, and does not collect your ESO gameplay data or account credentials.

1. Information we collect

DataWhen / why
Device identifier (a hashed machine ID)Only if you activate Pro — used to bind and validate your license so it works on your device(s). It is not used to track you across the web.
License key & activation statusTo verify your Pro entitlement with our licensing service when the app starts or you activate a key.
Email address & payment detailsCollected by our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, when you buy Pro. We do not receive or store your full card details.
Email address (macOS waitlist)Only if you voluntarily join the macOS waitlist — used solely to notify you when a macOS version is available.
Your "Describe" search textIf you use the Pro AI search, the text you type is sent to our backend and AI providers to return matching add-ons. Don't include personal information in search queries.
Basic request dataWhen the app checks for updates or the add-on catalog, standard network requests are made to GitHub, ESOUI, and our services, which may log IP addresses and timestamps for security and reliability.

2. How we use information

  • To provide and maintain the Software (catalog, installs, updates).
  • To validate Pro licenses and prevent abuse of paid features.
  • To process purchases (via Lemon Squeezy) and provide support.
  • To operate the AI "Describe" search (Pro) and improve result quality.
  • To notify macOS waitlist subscribers, if you opted in.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Who we share information with

We share information only with service providers that help us run the product:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payments and subscription management (Merchant of Record).
  • GitHub — hosting app downloads and update files.
  • ESOUI — the source of the add-on catalog and downloads.
  • Our licensing backend and AI providers — for license validation and, if you use it, the AI "Describe" search.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the security of our services.

4. Data retention

We keep license and purchase records for as long as needed to provide the Software, honor entitlements, comply with legal and accounting obligations, and resolve disputes. Waitlist emails are kept until the macOS version launches or you ask us to remove you. Search queries are retained only as long as needed to operate and improve the feature.

5. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email support@shoyru.app. You can also unsubscribe from the macOS waitlist at any time, and uninstall the Software to stop all local processing.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect information, including hashing device identifiers and relying on established providers (Lemon Squeezy, GitHub) for payments and distribution. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

The Software is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. International users

Our providers may process and store information in countries other than yours. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notified in the app.

10. Contact

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email support@shoyru.app.
